Diablo 4 Season 11 Endgame Guide

From Torment 1 to Pit 150 in one week. Step-by-step progression path.

← Back to All Guides

Season 11 of Diablo 4 brought a complete endgame overhaul. The Pit dungeon system now goes to level 200, Helltide events have new reward tiers, and Torment difficulty scaling received a full rework. Here is a step-by-step progression path that gets you from fresh level 60 to Pit 150 in about 7 days of focused play.

Day 1-2: Torment 1 Foundation

Your first priority after hitting 60 is getting a complete set of Ancestral gear.

Step 1: Run Helltide Events

Torment 1 Helltide drops Ancestral items at a decent rate. Focus on opening Tortured Gift chests (175 Aberrant Cinders each). Target full Ancestral gear in every slot before moving forward.

Step 2: Craft Core Legendary Aspects

Visit the Occultist and imprint the essential aspects for your build. Do not waste materials on perfect rolls yet. You need functional gear, not perfect gear.

Step 3: Push Nightmare Dungeons to Tier 30-40

This unlocks better Glyph XP and drops. Slot your key Paragon glyphs and level them to rank 15 minimum.

Day 3-4: Torment 2-3 Push

Once your build is functional with all core aspects and rank 15 glyphs:

Step 4: Switch to Torment 2

The jump from T1 to T2 is significant. If you can clear T1 content in under 3 minutes, you are ready.

Step 5: Farm The Pit Levels 30-60

This is where real progression happens. Pit rewards include:

  • Masterworking materials (essential for T3+)
  • Higher-tier Ancestral drops
  • Glyph XP (faster than NM dungeons)

Step 6: Masterwork Your Weapon First

Your weapon is your biggest damage multiplier. Get it to Masterwork rank 8 minimum. Each rank 4/8/12 gives a massive stat bonus that compounds with everything else.

Day 5-6: Breaking into Torment 4

This is where most players hit the wall. The jump to T4 requires:

  • All gear slots at Ancestral 800+ item power
  • Weapon at Masterwork rank 12
  • All glyphs at rank 21+
  • Correct Paragon board pathing (matters more than people think)

Step 7: Optimize Your Paragon Boards

The difference between a good and great Paragon setup is 30-40% damage. Use community-tested board layouts for your class. Small changes in node selection compound into massive power differences.

Step 8: Push Pit to Level 80-100

You should be clearing Pit 80 in under 4 minutes at this point. If not, your build has a bottleneck. Check defensive layers first, then damage rotation.

Day 7: Pit 100-150

Step 9: Target-Farm Unique Items

Some builds need specific Uber Uniques or Mythic Uniques to break the Pit 100+ wall. Know which bosses drop what you need and farm them efficiently.

Step 10: Masterwork Everything to Rank 12

This is expensive but necessary. Every piece of gear at MW12 adds another 10-15% total power.

Class Tier List for Pit Pushing (Season 11)

S Tier

Spiritborn - Still the undisputed king. Evade builds clear Pit 200. If you want the easiest path, play Spiritborn.

Necromancer - Minion builds are tanky and scale well past Pit 150. Very forgiving for mistakes.

A Tier

Sorcerer - Ball Lightning got buffed in Season 11. Top 3 for speed clears, slightly squishier than S tier.

Barbarian - Hammer of the Ancients is consistent to Pit 130. Tanky and reliable.

B Tier

Druid - Tornado builds are fun but cap around Pit 120 without perfect gear.

Rogue - Glass cannon. Great speed farming, struggles in high Pit without god-tier items.
