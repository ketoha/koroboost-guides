WoW Midnight Leveling Guide

80 to 90 in under 8 hours. Tested routes and XP optimization.

Midnight adds 10 levels (80-90) spread across Eversong Woods, the Ghostlands, and the restored Isle of Quel'Danas. The campaign takes roughly 12-14 hours if you do everything, but you can hit 90 in under 8 hours with the right approach. The Fastest Route: 80-90

Phase 1: Campaign Sprint (80-84)

Start the Midnight campaign immediately at 80. The intro scenario in Silvermoon gives about 40% of level 80 in 20 minutes. Follow the campaign through Eversong Woods until you finish the "Fall of the Sunwell" chapter (around level 83-84). Do NOT do any side quests during this phase. The campaign gives 3-4x more XP than side quests per time spent.

Pro tip: Pick up every bonus objective that is within 50 yards of your campaign quest path. Do not go out of your way for them, but if one overlaps with where you are going, the extra XP is free. There are 6 bonus objectives in Eversong that overlap perfectly with the main campaign path.

Phase 2: Dungeon + Campaign Hybrid (84-88)

At 84, you unlock all four leveling dungeons: Farstrider Lodge, Thalassian Depths, Ghostlands Bastion, and Elrendar Crossing. Queue for dungeons as DPS (or tank/healer for instant queues) and do campaign quests between queues. Each dungeon run gives approximately 60-70% of a level if you complete the dungeon quests (one-time quests inside each dungeon that give massive XP).

The dungeon quest XP breakdown:

  • Farstrider Lodge: 3 quests (kill boss, collect 8 pelts, find hidden chest) = 185K XP total
  • Thalassian Depths: 2 quests (kill final boss, destroy 3 void anchors) = 140K XP total
  • Ghostlands Bastion: 3 quests (kill all bosses, free 5 prisoners, loot Scourge artifact) = 195K XP total
  • Elrendar Crossing: 2 quests (kill final boss, escort NPC) = 130K XP total

After completing dungeon quests, regular dungeon runs still give good XP (about 40% of a level per run at this range). Keep queueing while doing campaign.

Phase 3: Final Push (88-90)

From 88 to 90, the campaign alone is not enough (it runs out of quests around halfway through 89). Fill the gap with:

  1. Ghostlands world quests (they unlock at 88 and give 35-45K XP each)
  2. The Isle of Quel'Danas weekly quest chain (available at 88, gives 250K XP)
  3. Dungeon spam if you have a fast group

XP Multipliers

War Mode (+15% XP)

Turn on War Mode in Stormwind or Orgrimmar before starting. The Midnight zones are relatively safe for War Mode because most players are doing PvE content. The 15% bonus is multiplicative with other bonuses and easily saves you 1-2 hours over the leveling process. If you are on a high-population PvP server and keep dying, turn it off. Dying wastes more time than the 15% saves.

Heirloom Gear

Heirlooms scale to level 90 in Midnight and provide 5% XP per piece (helmet and shoulders only give XP bonus). With both, that is 10% bonus XP. Combined with War Mode, you have +25% XP from the start. Heirlooms are purchased from the guild vendor for 500g each if you do not already have them.

Darkmoon Faire

If Darkmoon Faire is active, ride the carousel for a 10% XP buff that lasts 1 hour. It stacks with everything. With War Mode + heirlooms + Darkmoon, you are at +35% XP. This is the fastest possible leveling setup.

Rested XP

Log out in an inn or capital city between sessions. Rested XP doubles quest and mob kill XP. If you log out for 8 hours, you get about 1.5 levels worth of rested XP. Use it for the 88-90 grind where XP feels slowest.

Class-Specific Tips

  • Tanks and Healers: Queue for dungeons constantly. Your queue is instant and dungeons are the best XP from 84+.
  • DPS with strong AoE: Pull 5-6 mobs at once during questing. Frost DK, Ret Paladin, and Balance Druid can mass-pull and quest faster than other specs.
  • Slow-leveling specs: Holy Priest, Mistweaver Monk, and Resto Shaman are painful for questing. Stay in dungeons or switch to a DPS spec while leveling.
  • Druids: Travel Form is by far the best movement ability for leveling. No mounting required, instant cast, works indoors in some areas.

Common Mistakes

  • Doing every side quest. Side quests give 40-60% less XP per time than campaign quests. Skip them.
  • Grinding rare mobs for XP. Rares give a one-time bonus (equivalent to about 1 quest), not repeatable XP.
  • Not picking up dungeon quests. The quests are inside the dungeon entrance area. If you skip them, you lose 130-195K XP per dungeon.
  • Leveling professions while leveling your character. Professions give zero XP. Do them at max level.
  • Flying to quest hubs instead of using the campaign auto-travel. The campaign quest NPCs teleport you between zones for free.

Alt Leveling

Your second character and beyond can skip the campaign entirely after completing it once. You unlock Threads of Fate (Midnight version) which lets you level purely through dungeons, world quests, and bonus objectives. This is actually faster for alts because you can dungeon spam from level 80 with instant queues as a tank or healer. Expected time for an alt: 5-6 hours.
