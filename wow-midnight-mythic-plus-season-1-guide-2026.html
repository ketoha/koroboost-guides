WoW Midnight Mythic+ Season 1 Complete Guide (2026)

Mythic+ in WoW Midnight Season 1 brings 8 dungeons, a refreshed affix system, and Hero/Myth track gear up to ilvl 272. Whether you're pushing Keystone Master or farming weekly vaults, here's everything you need to know.

Season 1 Dungeon Pool

Midnight Season 1 splits the dungeon pool between four new Midnight dungeons and four returning legacy dungeons:

New Midnight Dungeons

  • Magisters' Terrace -- Silvermoon-themed arcane fortress with tight corridors and caster-heavy pulls
  • Maisara Caverns -- Underground troll network beneath Zul'Aman, heavy AoE required
  • Nexus-Point Xenas -- Void-touched dimensional rift with punishing mechanics on Tyrannical
  • Windrunner Spire -- Windrunner family estate, lots of verticality and line-of-sight pulls

Legacy Dungeons

  • Algeth'ar Academy (Dragonflight)
  • Seat of the Triumvirate (Legion)
  • Skyreach (Warlords of Draenor)
  • Pit of Saron (Wrath of the Lich King)

Affix System

  • +2: Lindormi's Guidance (training affix, removed at +6)
  • +5: Xal'atath's Bargain (weekly rotation: Ascendant, Voidbound, Pulsar, Devour)
  • +7: Tyrannical or Fortified (alternating)
  • +10: Both Tyrannical and Fortified active
  • +12: Xal'atath's Guile replaces Bargain

Getting Keystone Master (2000 Rating)

  • Time all 8 dungeons on at least +10 for base ~2000 rating
  • Both Tyrannical and Fortified completions count separately
  • Higher keys and faster times earn bonus rating

Tips for Pushing Higher Keys

  1. Route planning matters more than raw DPS. Bad pulls on Fortified +12 are wipes.
  2. Defensives over damage. Dead players do 0 DPS.
  3. Weekly affix awareness. Devour week is significantly harder.
  4. Great Vault strategy: Run 8 dungeons for 3 vault slots. Even failed keys count.
