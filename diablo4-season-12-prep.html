Diablo 4 Season 12 Preparation

What to do before reset. Complete checklist for a fast Season 12 start. Updated March 2026.

Season 11 is winding down, and Blizzard's already teasing Season 12 changes. If you've been grinding Helltide and pushing Tier 100 dungeons, you're probably wondering what matters going into the reset and what's just wasted effort.

Here's the truth: not everything you do in the final weeks of a season carries over. Some prep is actually worth your time, and some is just busywork. Let's break it down.

What Actually Carries Over to Season 12

Carries Over: Characters

At season reset, your existing seasonal characters become permanent. If you've been leveling an alt and want to play it immediately in Season 12, now's the time to get it to 100 and gear it up. You'll skip the leveling process entirely.

What to do: Rush any character you want to use in S12 to level 100. Don't waste time on perfecting gear -- that's what Season 12 is for.

Carries Over: Aspect Codex (Permanent Library)

This is the most valuable resource you can build up before reset. Every unique or class-specific aspect you've extracted stays forever. In Season 12, you can craft these aspects onto your gear instantly without finding them again.

What to do: Farm dungeons and Helltide for high-value aspects your planned build will use. Extract them to your codex. This is free power for S12.

What Doesn't Carry Over (Don't Waste Time)

Gone: Seasonal Materials

Forget about your Forgotten Souls, Demonic Hides, Baleful Fragments. Gone. Wiped. Don't farm endlessly trying to fill your stash with resources -- you'll get new ones in S12 immediately.

Gone: Gold (Kind Of)

You lose your seasonal gold, but honestly, gold is trivially easy to farm in Season 12. Don't bother optimizing your current gold stash.

Pre-Season 12 Checklist

1-2 Weeks Before Reset: The Grind Phase

  • Pick your S12 main build. Blizzard usually shares patch notes 1-2 weeks early. Read them. Don't commit to a build that's getting nerfed.
  • Farm aspects for that build. Run dungeons and Helltide explicitly hunting those aspects. Extract them the moment you get them.
  • Level any alts you want to use immediately. Get them to 100. Don't gear them -- just cap level.
  • Farm key uniques if you need them. If your build requires a specific Unique weapon, grind for it now.

Final Days Before Reset

  • Double-check your aspect codex. Log into each character class and verify the aspects you care about are extracted.
  • Check patch notes one more time. Sometimes day-of hotfixes happen. Adapt your prep if a mechanic changes.
  • Clean your inventory. Vendor your trash. You don't want clutter when S12 starts.
  • Pick your starting zone. Some zones are better for early farming depending on your build.

Pro Tips for Season 12 Kickoff

Have Your Build Map Ready

Know your skill allocation before you hit 50. Wasting points on the wrong skills slows your leveling massively. Print your passive tree or keep a second monitor with a guide ready.

Farm Aspect Drops, Not Gear

Your Season 12 gear will be outdated by hour three. What won't be outdated is your aspect codex. Prioritize farming for aspects in the first week, not perfect rolled gear.

Don't Get Attached to Season 11 Builds

Things change. Builds that were cracked in S11 might be mid in S12. Stay flexible. Have a backup plan if your main idea doesn't work out.
