WoW Midnight Mythic+ Season 1 Complete Guide: Dungeons, Loot & Tips (2026)

A guide by Koroboost

Mythic+ in WoW Midnight Season 1 brings 8 dungeons, a refreshed affix system, and Hero/Myth track gear up to ilvl 272. Whether you're pushing Keystone Master or farming weekly vaults, here's everything you need to know.

Season 1 Dungeon Pool

Midnight Season 1 splits the dungeon pool between four new Midnight dungeons and four returning legacy dungeons:

New Midnight Dungeons:

  • Magisters' Terrace -- Silvermoon-themed arcane fortress with tight corridors and caster-heavy pulls
  • Maisara Caverns -- Underground troll network beneath Zul'Aman, heavy AoE required
  • Nexus-Point Xenas -- Void-touched dimensional rift with punishing mechanics on Tyrannical
  • Windrunner Spire -- Windrunner family estate, lots of verticality and line-of-sight pulls

Legacy Dungeons:

  • Algeth'ar Academy (Dragonflight)
  • Seat of the Triumvirate (Legion)
  • Skyreach (Warlords of Draenor)
  • Pit of Saron (Wrath of the Lich King)

Loot & Gear Rewards

The loot system follows Midnight's gear track progression:

| Key Level | End-of-Dungeon | Great Vault | Track |

|-----------|---------------|-------------|-------|

| +2 to +3 | ilvl 249-252 | ilvl 259 | Champion/Hero |

| +4 to +5 | ilvl 255-259 | ilvl 263 | Hero 2/6 |

| +6 | ilvl 259 | ilvl 266 | Hero 3/6 |

| +7 to +9 | ilvl 262-266 | ilvl 269 | Hero 4/6 |

| +10+ | ilvl 266 | ilvl 272 | Myth 1/6 |

The vault caps at Myth 1/6 (272) from +10 keys. Running +12 or +15 doesn't increase vault ilvl but contributes more to your Mythic+ rating.

Affix System in Midnight

Affixes scale with key level this season:

  • +2: Lindormi's Guidance (training wheels, removed at +6)
  • +5: Xal'atath's Bargain (weekly rotation: Ascendant, Voidbound, Pulsar, Devour)
  • +7: Tyrannical OR Fortified (alternating weeks)
  • +10: Both Tyrannical AND Fortified active
  • +12: Xal'atath's Guile replaces Bargain (harder version)

Getting Keystone Master (2000 Rating)

To earn KSM and the exclusive Calamitous Carrion mount:

  • Time all 8 dungeons on at least +10 (gives base ~2000 rating)
  • Higher keys and faster times earn bonus rating
  • Both Tyrannical and Fortified completions count separately

Most players achieve KSM by timing +10-12 across all dungeons in both fortified and tyrannical weeks.

Tips for Pushing Higher Keys

  • Route planning matters more than raw DPS. Bad pulls on Fortified +12 are wipes.
  • Defensives > damage. Dead players do 0 DPS.
  • Weekly affix awareness. Xal'atath's Devour week is significantly harder -- plan accordingly.
  • Great Vault strategy: Run 8 dungeons per week for 3 vault slots. Even failed keys count.

Need Help With Your Keys?

If you're stuck on progression or just want fast weekly vault slots, professional Mythic+ carry services can help. Experienced teams run keys +2 through +15 with selfplay or piloted options, typically completing each dungeon in 20-40 minutes.
