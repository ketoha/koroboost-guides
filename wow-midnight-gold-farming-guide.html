WoW Midnight Gold Farming Guide

Best methods to make 500K+ gold per week in Season 1. Updated March 2026.

Gold farming in WoW Midnight has changed dramatically compared to previous expansions. With the new profession system overhaul and revamped material economy, some methods that worked in Dragonflight are now obsolete while new opportunities have emerged. After tracking gold-making methods across hundreds of characters on our Koroboost carry team, here are the most reliable ways to hit 500K+ gold per week.

1. Profession Crafting Orders

The crafting order system in Midnight received major updates. High-skill crafters earn 5,000-15,000 gold per order for endgame gear. The key professions for profit:

Top Money Makers
  • Blacksmithing - Mythic-quality plate armor sells for 8K-12K per piece. Demand is constant from warrior and paladin mains.
  • Alchemy - Flasks and combat potions sell at 200-400g each. Every raid night, millions are consumed.
Strong Earners
  • Leatherworking - Agility gear is always in demand due to the rogue/hunter meta in Season 1.
  • Enchanting - Weapon enchants for the new stat system are goldmines at 2,000-5,000g per enchant.

To maximize crafting profit, you need rank 5 in your specialization. This takes 2-3 weeks of focused knowledge point farming through weekly quests and first-craft bonuses.

2. Raw Gold Farming

Midnight dungeons drop significantly more raw gold than Dragonflight content. The best spots:

  • Silvermoon Court trash - 800-1,200 gold per clear in Heroic mode. Fast respawns make this farmable.
  • Old raid solos - Mythic Vault of the Incarnates now drops 3,500g per full clear. 7 raids = 24K passive gold per week.
  • World quests - The new Midnight world quest system rewards 500-800g for 5-minute quests.

3. Auction House Flipping

Material prices fluctuate heavily on Tuesday reset days. The strategy is simple: buy cheap materials Sunday-Monday, sell Wednesday-Thursday when raiders need supplies.

4. Mythic+ Carries

If you are skilled enough to carry Mythic+ keys, this is the fastest gold method. A +10 carry goes for 15,000-25,000g depending on your server. Teams running 4-man carries can split 50,000-80,000g per hour.

5. Weekly Lockout Optimization

Don't forget your weekly lockouts. Each character should complete:

  • Great Vault (3 M+ dungeons minimum for max reward slots)
  • World Boss (guaranteed gold + chance at BoE)
  • Timewalking events when available
  • Profession weekly quests (knowledge points = future profit)

A single character doing all weeklies generates roughly 50,000-80,000 gold per week passively. With 3 alts, that is 200K-320K from weeklies alone.

The Fastest Path to 500K/Week

Combine crafting orders with AH flipping on your main, run weekly lockouts on 2-3 alts, and you will consistently hit 500K+ without any mindless grinding. Pure farming is less efficient but more consistent for players who prefer a simpler approach.
