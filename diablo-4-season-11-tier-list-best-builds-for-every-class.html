Diablo 4 Season 11 Tier List: Best Builds for Every Class Ranked

A guide by Koroboost

# Diablo 4 Season 11 Tier List: Best Builds for Every Class Ranked Season 11 is actually pretty well-balanced for Diablo 4. Every class is viable, which means the tier list is less "play this or you're bad" and more "here's what's performing best right now." The meta shifted since launch, and some classes got serious buffs that nobody expected. ## Class Overview **Necromancer**: Still king. Corpse explosion builds are cracked, summoner builds are braindead overpowered, and blood surge is a legitimate alternative if you're sick of the meta. **Barbarian**: Got huge buffs in the last patch. Bleed builds and hammer builds are actually competitive now. Wild to see barbs topping damage meters. **Sorceress**: Still solid but not as dominant as she was. Frost builds are reliable, fire is glass cannon but deals insane damage. **Rogue**: Dagger builds slap. Crit-based stuff is cracked. Cold Imbuement is the best imbuement by far. **Druid**: Lupine Feast is beyond broken right now. Companion builds are also viable. Honestly underrated class. ## S-Tier: What You Play To Win **Necromancer - Corpse Explosion**: The DPS is absolutely cracked. This is the ceiling for damage in the game right now. If you want to push pit tiers, you're running this or a summoner build. The playstyle is pretty brain-dead too. Cast something, explode corpses, everything dies. Huge damage, low effort. This is the king build for endgame farming. **Barbarian - Bleed Hammer**: Came out of nowhere in the last patch. Bleed synergies got reworked and suddenly barbs are dealing competitive DPS. The playstyle is all about standing there and hitting things, which is on-brand for barbs. This is way less flashy than corpse explosion but it feels incredibly powerful in actual gameplay. Crit multiplier scaling is insane. **Necromancer - Summoner**: The lazy man's way to endgame. Summon skeletons, let them kill everything, farm pit tiers passively. Not as much raw DPS as corpse explosion but way more chill. If you want to farm endlessly without caring, this is your build. The AI is terrible and half your skeletons will be useless, but it still works. ## A-Tier: Competitive and Solid **Sorceress - Frozen Orb**: Classic reliable build. Good damage, decent survivability, smooth gameplay loop. Not flashy but consistent. Pros: Freezes everything, easy to gear, survives most stuff. Cons: Doesn't have the same damage ceiling as corpse explosion. **Rogue - Cold Dagger Crit**: Dagger rework made this actually good now. Crit scaling is insane and Cold Imbuement multiplies your damage even more. The playstyle is all about weaving in and out of range, crit-fishing, and deleting everything with execute damage. **Druid - Lupine Feast Companion**: This build slaps way harder than people realize. The damage multipliers on Lupine Feast are absolutely unhinged. Your companions do most of the work while you hit things casually. Underrated because most people sleep on companion scaling. The build is actually busted if you gear it properly. **Barbarian - Upheaval**: AoE bleed everything. Plays like a traditional Diablo barbarian: run in, smash, run out. The damage is legit good now. Less efficient than hammer bleed for single-target, but the clear speed on packs is insane. ## B-Tier: Good, Not Meta **Sorceress - Inferno**: High damage but glass cannon. If you're geared enough, the DPS is nutty. If you mess up positioning, you're dead instantly. Fun to play if you like punishment. Requires more skill than most builds. **Rogue - Poison Imbuement Crit**: Poison damage is actually viable now. The playstyle is more complex than cold because you're managing DOT damage and crit separately. Works but doesn't outperform cold dagger by enough to recommend unless you specifically like poison gameplay. **Necromancer - Blood Surge**: The hybrid playstyle is cool. You're doing sustained damage plus corpse explosion. More interactive than corpse explosion only, slightly less damage. Fun for people who think standard corpse explosion is braindead. Which it is. **Druid - Pulverize Earthen Bulwark**: Tanky and deals solid damage. The synergy between pulverize and earthquake is legit good. Not the fastest clear but very safe. Great for casual farming where you don't want to pay attention. ## C-Tier: Playable, Not Recommended **Sorceress - Firewall**: High damage but weird positioning requirements. The setup time is annoying. You're channeling to place walls then running away. It works but it's clunky. **Rogue - Grenade Build**: AoE is good but single target is weak. Works for dungeons, struggles in pit tiers against unique monsters. **Barbarian - Whirlwind**: Feels nostalgic for D2 players. Actual damage is mediocre compared to bleed builds. You're doing it because it feels good, not because it performs. **Druid - Tornado Shred**: Tornado itself is fine but the crit scaling doesn't hit as hard as other druid builds. Playable but outclassed. **Necromancer - Bone Prison Control**: Very niche. Works if you want a control build. Damage is significantly lower than corpse explosion. For meme runs only. ## Pit Tier Scaling If you're pushing pit tiers specifically, these are your builds: **T1-T25**: Anything works. Gear doesn't even matter. **T25-T50**: Meta builds start mattering. You need bleed barb, corpse necro, or cold rogue minimum. **T50-T75**: You're committed to one build. Corpse explosion and summoner are the safe choices because you scale the hardest. **T75+**: Only corpse explosion necro and gear-checked barb survive. If you're pushing these tiers, you're playing a very specific build. ## Seasonal Changes Season 11 rebalanced a ton of stuff: - Bleed scaling got way better (barbarian benefit) - Cold imbuement multiplier increased (rogue benefit) - Companion damage got reworked (druid benefit) - Corpse explosion stayed broken (necromancer status quo) This is honestly one of the most balanced seasons yet. The gap between S-tier and A-tier builds is smaller than before. ## Gearing Priority For season 11: **Offense First**: Damage is way more important than defense. If you die, you're not farming. If you don't kill fast, you're wasting time. **Crit Multiplier is King**: Everything scales off crit multiplier. Stack it aggressively. **Imbuements Matter**: Pick an imbuement and scale it. Don't try to do everything. **Resistances After Offense**: You need like 50+ all res and you're fine. Anything more is overkill unless you're pushing high pit tiers. ## Farm vs Push Season 11 farming meta (pit T30-T50): - Corpse necro for speed - Summoner for AFK farming - Cold rogue for chill gameplay Push meta (pit T75+): - Corpse necro only - Maybe bleed barb if you're cracked at gearing Farming is about clear speed. Pushing is about damage scaling. ## New Player Recommendation If you're starting season 11 fresh, play Necromancer Corpse Explosion. Not because it's fun, but because: 1. It's overpowered 2. It scales with minimal gear 3. You can farm endlessly and progress 4. Once you have currency, you can respec to whatever you want Start there, farm for a week, then pick your actual character if you get bored. ## Class Viability Real talk: every class is viable for endgame. Even the "worst" builds can do pit T50. The difference between S-tier and B-tier is clear speed, not viability. Pick the build that looks fun to you. If you want to optimize later, the respec cost is low enough that it doesn't matter. ## Boosts and Services If you want to experience high-tier farming without the grind, there are endgame farming services available.
