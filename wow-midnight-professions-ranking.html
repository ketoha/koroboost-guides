Choosing the right professions in WoW Midnight can mean the difference between struggling for gold and sitting comfortably with millions. This ranking is based on actual income data from Season 1, tracked across our Koroboost team of professional players, not theoretical potential.

S Tier: Consistent High Income

1. Alchemy (S+)

Flasks, potions, and phials are consumed every raid night by millions of players. Midnight introduced new combat potions that sell for 300-500g each. The beauty of Alchemy is consistency: demand never drops.

Weekly income: 100K-200K per character

2. Enchanting (S)

Every gear upgrade needs enchants. The new Midnight stat system added two new enchant slots, increasing demand across the board. Weapon enchants sell for 2,000-5,000g each.

Weekly income: 80K-150K per character

A Tier: Strong but Seasonal

3. Blacksmithing (A+)

Plate armor crafting orders are extremely profitable early in patches. Income drops as the season progresses and more crafters reach max skill. Best for warriors, paladins, and death knights who can craft their own gear too.

Weekly income: 60K-120K

4. Jewelcrafting (A)

Gems are always needed, and Midnight's socket system guarantees steady demand. The new prismatic gem recipes require high skill but sell for 3,000-6,000g each.

Weekly income: 50K-100K

5. Leatherworking (A)

Mail and leather armor for the dominant rogue/hunter specs. Season 1 meta heavily favors agility classes, which keeps demand high.

Weekly income: 50K-90K

How to Level Professions Fast

  1. Focus knowledge points on one specialization tree first
  2. Complete all first-craft bonuses (one-time large XP gains)
  3. Do weekly Artisan's Consortium quests
  4. Use Darkmoon Faire buff when available (+5 skill)
  5. Buy cheap materials on weekends for leveling crafts
