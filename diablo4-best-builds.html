Season 11 Build Tier List

Season 11 shook up the meta with tempering overhauls and class rebalancing. Spiritborn is still king, but Necromancer got serious buffs that push Bone Spear into S-tier territory. Here is where every class stands right now.

S-Tier

Spiritborn - Quill Volley

Fastest clear speed in the game. Quill Volley with Eagle aspect deletes entire screens in under a second. Pit 150 in under 3 minutes. The Season 11 tempering changes barely affected this build because it scales off flat damage.

  • Key aspects: Eagle Flight, Razor Wings, Soaring Assault
  • Temper priority: attack speed, crit damage, movement speed

Necromancer - Bone Spear

15% damage buff this season pushed Bone Spear firmly into S-tier. Single target is unmatched. Bosses melt in 2-3 seconds at high Pit levels. Requires precise positioning but the burst is insane.

  • Key aspects: Splintering, Exposed Flesh
  • Temper priority: crit damage, vulnerable damage, core skill damage
A-Tier

Druid - Tornado

AoE king this season. Reworked Tempest Roar makes tornado generation insane: 8-12 per cast in density. Pit 130-140 comfortably, 150 with perfect gear.

Barbarian - Bash

Tankiest build in D4. Face-tanks Pit 150 bosses without dodging. Damage is lower but you literally cannot die. Great for players who hate mechanics.

B-Tier

Sorcerer - Chain Lightning

Bounces buffed from 5 to 7. Screen lights up and everything dies. Not S-tier speed but extremely satisfying. Pit 120-130.

Rogue - Twisting Blades

Highest skill-cap build. In good hands it competes with A-tier. Average hands, C-tier. Season 11 dodge buff helps survivability.

Gearing Tips

  • Tempering > item level. One perfect temper beats 5 item levels.
  • Elixirs in Pits. Match resistance elixir to Pit damage type.
  • Masterwork order: weapon first, amulet, gloves.
  • Skip mythic uniques unless the roll is perfect. Well-tempered legendaries win.
