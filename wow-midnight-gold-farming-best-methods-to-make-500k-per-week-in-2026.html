Title: WoW Midnight Gold Farming: Best Methods to Make 500K+ Per Week in 2026

Gold farming in WoW Midnight has changed dramatically compared to previous expansions. With the new profession system overhaul and revamped material economy, some methods that worked in Dragonflight are now obsolete while new opportunities have emerged.

After tracking gold-making methods across hundreds of characters, here are the most reliable ways to hit 500K+ gold per week in Midnight Season 1.

1. Profession Crafting Orders

The crafting order system in Midnight received major updates. High-skill crafters can now earn 5,000-15,000 gold per order for endgame gear. The key professions for profit:

  • Blacksmithing -- Mythic-quality plate armor sells for 8K-12K per piece
  • Leatherworking -- Agility gear is always in demand due to the rogue/hunter meta
  • Alchemy -- Flasks and combat potions remain steady sellers at 200-400g each
  • Enchanting -- Weapon enchants for the new stat system are goldmines

To maximize crafting profit, you need rank 5 in your specialization. This takes 2-3 weeks of focused knowledge point farming.

2. Raw Gold Farming

Midnight dungeons drop significantly more raw gold than Dragonflight content. The best spots:

  • Silvermoon Court trash -- 800-1,200 gold per clear in Heroic mode
  • Old raid solos -- Mythic Vault of the Incarnates now drops 3,500g per full clear
  • World quests -- The new Midnight world quest system rewards 500-800g for 5-minute quests

3. Auction House Flipping

Material prices fluctuate heavily on Tuesday reset days. Buy cheap materials Sunday-Monday, sell Wednesday-Thursday when raiders need supplies. Key items to flip:

  • Midnight Herbs (price swings 40-60%)
  • Enchanting materials (steady 30% margins)
  • Consumables before raid reset

4. Mythic+ Carries

If you're skilled enough to carry Mythic+ keys, this is the fastest gold method. A +10 carry goes for 15,000-25,000g depending on your server. Teams running 4-man carries can split 50,000-80,000g per hour.

For players who want to skip the grind entirely, professional services like Koroboost offer gold farming and carry services that save hundreds of hours.

5. Weekly Lockout Optimization

Don't forget your weekly lockouts. Each character should complete:

  • Great Vault (3 M+ dungeons minimum)
  • World Boss
  • Timewalking events when available
  • Profession weekly quests

A single character doing all weeklies generates roughly 50,000-80,000 gold per week passively.

Bottom Line

The fastest path to 500K/week is combining crafting orders with AH flipping. Pure farming is less efficient but more consistent. If you're running multiple characters, the passive weekly income alone can hit your target.

Title: Most Profitable Professions in WoW Midnight: Complete Ranking for Season 1

Choosing the right professions in WoW Midnight can mean the difference between struggling for gold and sitting comfortably with millions. This ranking is based on actual income data from Season 1, not theoretical potential.

S Tier: Consistent High Income

1. Alchemy (S+)

Flasks, potions, and phials are consumed every raid night by millions of players. Midnight introduced new combat potions that sell for 300-500g each. Weekly income potential: 100K-200K per character.

2. Enchanting (S)

Every gear upgrade needs enchants. The new Midnight stat system added two new enchant slots, increasing demand. Weapon enchants sell for 2,000-5,000g. Weekly: 80K-150K.

A Tier: Strong but Seasonal

3. Blacksmithing (A+)

Plate armor crafting orders are extremely profitable early in patches. Income drops as the season progresses. Weekly: 60K-120K.

4. Jewelcrafting (A)

Gems are always needed, and Midnight's socket system guarantees steady demand. Weekly: 50K-100K.

5. Leatherworking (A)

Mail and leather armor for the dominant rogue/hunter specs. Weekly: 50K-90K.

B Tier: Decent Supplemental Income

6. Inscription -- Missives and Vantus Runes. Weekly: 30K-60K.

7. Tailoring -- Cloth armor and bags. Weekly: 20K-50K.

8. Engineering -- Niche items, goggles. Weekly: 15K-40K.

C Tier: Gathering (Passive)

9. Herbalism -- Sell raw materials. Weekly: 20K-40K.

10. Mining -- Less demand than herbs. Weekly: 15K-30K.

11. Skinning -- Lowest demand gathering prof. Weekly: 10K-25K.

How to Level Professions Fast

The fastest way to max your professions:

  • Focus knowledge points on one specialization tree
  • Complete all first-craft bonuses (one-time large XP)
  • Do weekly profession quests (Artisan's Consortium)
  • Use Darkmoon Faire buff when available (+5 skill)

Getting to max rank in your chosen specialization takes about 2-3 weeks of daily play. If you want to fast-track profession leveling, services like Koroboost's profession powerleveling can get you to max rank in days instead of weeks.

Best Profession Combos by Class

  • Warriors/Paladins/DKs: Blacksmithing + Mining (self-sufficient plate crafting)
  • Rogues/Monks/Druids/DHs: Leatherworking + Skinning
  • Mages/Warlocks/Priests: Tailoring + Enchanting (DE your own gear)
  • Any class (pure gold focus): Alchemy + Herbalism

The Real Money Move

The highest earners don't just craft. They craft + flip materials + fill niche orders. A player running Alchemy + Enchanting on their main, with 2-3 alts doing weekly lockouts, can consistently generate 400K-600K gold per week without any grinding.
