M+ Affix Guide: Midnight Season 1

Every affix explained in plain English. What they do, how to handle them, and which weeks to push.

Affixes separate M+ from regular dungeons. Every keystone level adds modifiers that change how the entire dungeon plays. The difference between a smooth key and a disaster often comes down to understanding what the affixes do and adjusting your play.

How Affixes Work

Key LevelAffixes Active
+2 to +31 affix (Level 2)
+4 to +62 affixes (Level 2 + Level 4)
+7 to +93 affixes (Level 2 + Level 4 + Level 7)
+10+4 affixes (all above + seasonal)

Affixes rotate weekly on Tuesday reset. Some weeks are push weeks, some are "just do your vault" weeks.

Level 2 Active on All Keys

Fortified

Trash mobs: +20% health, +30% damage

Trash is already the hardest part of M+. Fortified makes it harder. Boss fights feel the same.

  • Pull smaller packs. No hero pulls on Fortified weeks.
  • Use defensive cooldowns on trash, not just bosses
  • Interrupt EVERYTHING. Annoying casts become lethal.
  • Healers: save mana for trash, bosses are easy on Fort

Tyrannical

Bosses: +40% health, +15% damage

Boss fights become long endurance tests. 90-second bosses become 2+ minute fights. More time = more mechanics = more chances to wipe.

  • Save cooldowns for bosses, not trash
  • Lust on the hardest boss, not the first boss
  • DPS: sustained damage matters more than burst
  • Healers: bosses drain mana. Drink between phases.

Which is easier? Most players prefer Fortified -- trash is controllable (pull less). Tyrannical bosses are binary: either you have the DPS or you don't. Less room to adjust.

Level 4 Affixes

Bolstering

When a non-boss dies, nearby enemies get +20% health and damage for 20s. Stacks.

Kill 4 small mobs before the big one? The big mob now has +80% health and damage. That's basically a boss.

  • Kill mobs at the same rate. Cleave and AoE are your friend.
  • Don't focus-fire small mobs and leave the big one alive
  • Pull packs with similar health pools together

Bursting

When a non-boss dies, everyone takes a stacking DoT for 4 seconds.

Kill 5 mobs at once? That's 5 stacks on the whole group. At 4+ stacks, healers barely keep up. At 6+, people die.

  • Stagger kills. Don't kill everything at once.
  • Stop DPS at 4 stacks, let them fall off
  • DPS: use personal defensives and self-healing

Raging

Non-boss enemies enrage at 30% health: +50% damage until dead.

  • Kill low-health mobs fast. Don't leave them at 30% and switch targets.
  • Tanks: prepare a defensive when mobs enrage
  • Hunters and Druids can Soothe the enrage (removes it)

Spiteful

Dead non-bosses spawn a Shade that fixates a random player. Massive melee damage. Dies after 15 seconds.

  • Run away. Shades are slow. Just kite them.
  • Ranged DPS: barely affects you. Sidestep.
  • Melee DPS: your nightmare. Be ready to disengage after every kill.
  • Don't bother taunting shades. Let fixated player kite.

Level 7 Affixes

Explosive

Trash mobs spawn Explosive Orbs. If not killed in 6 seconds, massive party-wide damage.

  • Assign one person (usually ranged) to orb duty
  • Orbs have low health (1-2 hits) but spawn constantly during big pulls
  • Macro: /target Explosive Orb for fast targeting
  • The danger is 3-4 orbs during a chaotic pull when nobody's watching

Grievous

Players below 90% health get a stacking bleed. Stops only when healed above 90%.

  • Healers: keep everyone topped. Below 90% is the danger zone.
  • DPS: use self-heals, health pots, healthstones
  • Snowballs FAST. One person at 60% with 4 stacks bleeds out in seconds.
  • Versatility and Stamina matter more on Grievous weeks

Volcanic

Random volcanic plumes erupt under ranged players. Heavy damage + knockup.

  • Move when you see the ground glow. That's it.
  • Only affects players 5+ yards from the mob. Melee is safe.
  • Ranged/healers: stay mobile, don't hardcast near mobs
  • Real danger: getting knocked into another pack or off a ledge

Push Weeks vs Skip Weeks

Best Weeks to Push

  • Fortified + any non-Bolstering Level 4
  • Tyrannical + Bursting (trash is easy, just stagger kills)

Worst Weeks (Just Do Your Vault)

  • Tyrannical + Bolstering (everything is hard)
  • Fortified + Spiteful + Grievous (trash is a nightmare)

Check the affix rotation on raider.io every Tuesday. Plan your pushes around good weeks.

The Affix Mindset

Affixes aren't punishment. They're modifiers that change how you approach the dungeon. Players who adjust weekly push higher keys than players who run the same way regardless of affixes.

Read the affixes before you queue. Adjust your pulls, cooldowns, and expectations.

If you want to see how experienced players handle brutal affix weeks, running M+ with professional teams shows you exactly when they pull smaller, save cooldowns, and adjust routes based on the weekly combo.
