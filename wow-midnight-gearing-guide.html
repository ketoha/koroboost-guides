Midnight Season 1 uses a gear upgrade system similar to The War Within but with some significant changes. The item level ceiling is higher, crafted gear is more important, and there is a new currency (Sunwell Remnants) that acts as a catch-up mechanic. Here is the full breakdown of how to gear from fresh level 90 to raid-ready as fast as possible.

Week 1 Gearing Plan (Fresh 90)

Day 1-2: Get to 571 iLvl

At fresh 90, you are around 555 iLvl from quest rewards. Your immediate goal is 571, which unlocks Heroic dungeons. Do this:

  1. Complete all world quests in Eversong, Ghostlands, and Quel'Danas. Each gives 558 gear. Fill every slot.
  2. Run 3-4 Normal dungeons for 564 drops. Prioritize your weakest slots (usually trinkets and weapons).
  3. Buy a crafted weapon from the Auction House. Even a rank 3 crafted weapon (iLvl 590) is a massive upgrade and will be your biggest single power increase.
  4. Do the weekly Sunwell Remnants quest (kill 50 mobs in the Ghostlands). This gives 200 Remnants, enough to buy one 577 piece.

Day 3-4: Push to 580

Run Heroic dungeons until you are 577+, then start Mythic 0s. There are 8 Mythic 0 dungeons, each dropping 580 loot and each lockout resets weekly. Target dungeons that drop your best-in-slot trinkets and tier-adjacent slots first.

Crafted gear priority: Your first two crafted pieces should be your weapon and one of: chest, legs, or helm (whichever has your tier set). Crafted gear at rank 5 with an Enchanted Runic Crest can reach 616 iLvl, which is Heroic raid quality. This is a massive shortcut. Budget about 150K gold for two rank 5 crafted pieces on most servers.

Day 5-7: Mythic+ and Vault

Start running Mythic+ keys. Even +2 keys give 584 base loot and contribute to your Great Vault. The vault reward for completing 8 M+ dungeons at +10 or above is a 610 piece. This is the single most important weekly target for gearing.

The Great Vault

The vault offers up to 9 choices per week across three categories:

  • Raids: Kill 2 / 5 / 8 bosses for 1 / 2 / 3 vault options (item level based on difficulty)
  • M+: Complete 1 / 4 / 8 dungeons for 1 / 2 / 3 vault options (item level based on key level)
  • PvP: Earn 1250 / 2500 / 5000 honor for 1 / 2 / 3 vault options

Always aim for at least 8 M+ runs and a Normal/Heroic raid clear to maximize your vault choices. More choices = higher chance of getting your BiS item in the right slot.

Sunwell Remnants (Catch-Up Currency)

Sunwell Remnants drop from every activity in Midnight zones: world quests, dungeons, raids, rare mobs, and treasure chests. You can spend them at the Remnant Vendor in Silvermoon on 577 gear for any slot. This is the catch-up mechanic for alts and fresh characters. A full set of 577 gear costs about 2,500 Remnants, which takes roughly one week of casual play to farm. Not the fastest path, but it fills gaps if you are unlucky with drops.

Tier Set

Midnight tier sets drop from Sunwell Reclaimed (LFR, Normal, Heroic, Mythic) and from the Great Vault. The 2-piece bonus activates at 2 tier pieces, the 4-piece at 4. Most tier sets are significant power increases (10-15% DPS for DPS specs, 8-12% throughput for healers). Getting your 4-piece should be a top priority.

Tier tokens drop from specific bosses:

  • Helm token: Kael'thas Reborn (Boss 7)
  • Shoulder token: Lor'themar (Boss 5)
  • Chest token: Eredar Twins Reforged (Boss 8)
  • Glove token: Dar'Khan Drathir (Boss 3)
  • Leg token: M'uru's Echo (Boss 9, final boss)

Enchants and Gems

Do not skip enchants and gems. At 590+ iLvl, full enchants add roughly 3-4% total performance. That is the difference between timing a +10 and bricking it.

  • Weapon enchant: 60-80K gold. Non-negotiable, biggest single enchant.
  • Ring enchants (x2): 15-25K each. Always your primary stat.
  • Chest enchant: 10-15K gold. Stamina or primary stat depending on role.
  • Leg enchant: 20-30K gold. Crafted by leatherworkers.
  • Gems: 2-5K each, you need 3-4 sockets. Use your primary stat gems.

Common Gearing Mistakes

  • Ignoring crafted gear. Two rank 5 pieces at 616 iLvl instantly put you at Heroic raid level for those slots.
  • Not doing the Great Vault. Missing a vault week is the biggest gear setback possible.
  • Running only one type of content. Doing M+, raid, AND PvP gives you 9 vault choices instead of 3.
  • Upgrading items you will replace. Do not spend crests upgrading 584 gear when you can get 603 from Heroic raid.
  • Hoarding crests. Crests expire at the end of the season. Use them.
